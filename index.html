Jojo

Jojo's Bizarre Adventure is an exciting series about the history of the Joestar bloodline. Each part is characterized by new characters, new settings, and new genres of storytelling!

Whether the story is about training to defeat a villain, travelling the world on an adventure, finding a murderer in a small town, or fighting against the mafia, Jojo's Bizarre Adventure finds ways to keep itself fresh. On top of that, each part is characterized by a new lead character, each with their own personalities, powers, and friend group to join them on their adventure.

Like the name of the series suggests, it can become pretty bizarre.
