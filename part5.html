Golden Wind

Part 5 takes place in Italy, where Giorno Giovanna joins the mafia in order to take down the leader, who is known for his cruelty and questionable actions. Giorno joins one of the branches of the mafia led by Bruno Bucchiarati, and together, they must protect the boss' daughter and gain power in the mafia. The gang must face the boss' suboordinates, each with their own abilities in order to survive and discover the secret identity of the mob boss.

Important Characters:

Giorno Giovanna Giorno was orphaned as a kid, fending for himself until someone in the mafia made sure he was okay. When he grew up and saw how the mafia was affecting Italy, he vowed to join and gain power to change it from the inside.
Bruno Bucchiarati Bruno is the leader of one division of the mafia but joins Giorno on his mission to change the mafia. Their goal is to promote Bruno to a higher position, then remobe the current boss once they get close enough.
Guido Mista Guido is a member of Bruno's crew and has a unique stand, as his powers are having six small people to help him aim bullets, each with their own personality.
Leone Abbacchio Abbacchio is an ex police officer, who gave up trying to bring fair justice after his partner was killed, later joining Bruno's group. He is distrustful and cautious of people.
Narancia Ghriga Narancia never fit in growing up anywhere until he found Bruno's group. Using his stand power, Aerosmith, he can scout enemies using its radar.
Pannacotta Fugo Fugo grew up in a rich family but left it behind, joining the mafia. He is one of the smartest in the group and teaches Narancia how to read.
Trish Una Trish is the mafia boss' daughter, although she has never met him because the boss is secretive about his identity. She joins the crew on their mission across italy, and they defend her from her dad, who wants to capture her.
