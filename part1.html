Phantom Blood

Phantom Blood is the first part of the series and the introduction to the Joestar family. It follows a young Jonathan Joestar, who believes in chivalry and good character, and his rival, Dio Brando, who wants to gain power for himself and making Jonathan's life miserable. When Dio finds a mask that turns him into a vampire, it is up to Jonathan to defeat him before he does harm to others. Despite being the shortest part, Phantom Blood lays the groundwork of the rest of the series, as future events can be tied back to this part.

Important Characters:

Jonathan Joestar Jonathan is the main character, who grew up in a wealthy household and practices martial arts. He is Erina's significant other and believes in chivalry and following the rules.
Dio Brando Dio is the main antagonist and hates Jonathan. He was adopted by Jonathan's father, but he has plans to steal the family's fortune for himself using a mask that grants him powers
Erina Pendleton Erina is Jonathan's significant other and is one of the main reasons for his heroism
William Zepelli Zepelli is Jonathan's martial arts teacher, who trains him in a special breathing technique to defeat Dio.
Robert Speedwagon Speedwagon is a criminal-turned businessman who helps Jonathan throughout his adventure, and helps out the Joestar family across generations.
