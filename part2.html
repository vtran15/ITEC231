Battle Tendency

Battle Tendency follows the grandson of Jonathan Joestar, Joseph Joestar. Whereas Jonathan was chivalrous and followed the rules, Joseph is a rule breaker and is not afraid to fight dirty. The story follows Joseph and his friends as they travel across the world to defeat the Pillarmen, an ancient group of people with special powers, who created the mask that Dio used in part 1. It is a race to see who will obtain a relic first and whether or not the Pillarmen will evolve into a greater threat to the world.

Important Characters:

Joseph Joestar Joseph is the grandson of Jonathan, and grew up without his parents, raised by Erina. Joseph uses his wits and unpredictability to win his battles, no matter how underhanded the tactics.
Caesar Zepelli Caesar is the grandson of William Zepelli and is just as arrogant and hardheaded as Joseph. He trains alongside Joseph and gradually transition from enemies to friends.
Lisa Lisa Lisa Lisa is Joseph and Caesar's martial arts teacher, who they meet in Italy. She also knows about Joseph's history and what happened to his father.
The Pillarmen The Pillarmen are the main villains of this part. They are ancient beings who awaken in the 20th century, and want to gain power prove their superiority to humans and conquer their weakness, the sun. The 3 main Pillarmen are Kars, Esidesi, and Waamu.
Robert Speedwagon In this part, Speedwagon is an established businessman and founded the Speedwagon Foundation, which supplies money, technology, and supplies to Joseph on his journey across the world.
