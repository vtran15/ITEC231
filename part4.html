Diamond is Unbreakable

Diamond is Unbreakable takes place in the a small but crazy town called Morioh. It follows Josuke Higashikata, Joseph's secret child in Japan. Although the town is normal on the surface, it secretly holds many people with abilities granted by a stand arrow, and a serial killer, who the people of the town must work together to stop. Despite facing many enemy stand users, Josuke prefers to befriend them, rather than fighting them in order to have them contribute to finding the town's murderer.

Important Characters:

Josuke Higashikata Josuke is the secret child of Joseph Joestar and lives with his mom. He acts looks like a delinquent but has a kind heart until you insult his haircut. His power, Crazy Diamond allows him to heal or revert things back to their original state.
Okuyasu Nijimura Orginally an antagonist, Okuyasu joins sides with Josuke and eventually becomes his best friend and classmate. Okuyasu is hotheaded and often acts before thinking.
Koichi Hirose Koichi is one of Josuke's classmates who joins the adventure. He is shy and reserved but is respected among the group for being the most reliable.
Rohan Kishibe Rohan is a comic book artist who prefers not to interact with others, although is deeply invested in the murder cases, being one of the main people to contribute to discovering the killer's identity.
Jotaro Joestar After the events of part 3, Jotaro travels the world and finds himself in the town of Morioh to investigate the murder cases and find any connections to the stand arrow.
