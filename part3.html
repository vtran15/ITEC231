Stardust Crusaders

Stardust Crusaders features the return of the antagonist from the part one, Dio. It is up to Joseph Joestar and his grandson, the new protagonist of this part, Jotaro Joestar. This part follows them and their crew as they travel from Japan to Egypt to save Jotaro's mother as she awakens a power that slowly kills her. With their new powers, called stands, they must defeat other stand users using their wits.

Important Characters:

Jotaro Kujo Jotaro is the grandson of Joseph Joestar, and is a quiet and stoic person. He gained a stand power named Star Platinum, which he uses to defeat his enemies in order save his mother from her illness.
Joeseph Joestar Joeseph has grown since part 2, becoming a mentor and a veteran to lead the team to defeat Dio. With his newly obtained stand power, he can obtain information about their enemies as they travel across the world.
Noriyaki Kakyoin Kakyoin is a student alongside Jotaro, but joined on their journey to seek revenge on Dio after he brainwashed him.
Jean Pierre Polnareff Polnareff joined the crusaders after Dio brainwashed him. He is driven by his desire to find his sister's killer and avenge her.
Muhammad Avdol Avdol joined the crusaders alonside Joseph, and is a fortuneteller. He is knowledgeable on other people's stand powers.
Iggy Igi is a dog who joined the crusaders once they arrived in Egypt. He is rebellious and often wanders on his own. He often clashes with Polnareff.
Dio Dio returns in this part after waiting for 100 years for his chance to strike again. After awakening his stand, The World, he tries to regain his strength and defeat the Joestar bloodline by sending his followers to defeat them.
